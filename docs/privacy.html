Privacy Policy

隐私政策

CineCal 电影日历以本地优先的方式提供电影推荐与桌面小组件。我们不要求注册账号，不出售个人信息，也不使用跨应用追踪。

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 14 日

我们不收集的数据

CineCal 不会将姓名、电子邮箱、电话号码、精确位置、通讯录、照片、健康信息、广告标识符或使用分析数据发送给开发者。应用不包含广告、第三方分析 SDK 或行为追踪 SDK。

仅保存在设备上的信息

为了同步主应用与小组件并改善本地推荐，CineCal 会在设备的本地存储和系统钥匙串中保存：

  • 已打开或跳过的电影条目标识符；
  • 当天选择的电影条目标识符；
  • 日期语言、星期语言和农历显示设置。

这些信息不会上传给开发者，也不会用于追踪或与第三方共享。

网络请求与缓存

应用会从 CineCalData 的公开 GitHub 数据源和内容分发网络下载电影目录、推荐数据、向量文件和小组件图片。为减少流量，图片会临时缓存在设备上，并由应用按约 20 MB 的上限自动清理。网络服务提供商可能依据其自身政策处理连接所必需的技术信息，例如 IP 地址和请求时间。

第三方链接

当你主动点击电影卡片或小组件时，CineCal 会打开豆瓣的网页或应用。离开 CineCal 后，相关服务的数据处理受其自己的隐私政策约束。

联系我们

如对本政策或设备本地数据有疑问，请通过 CineCalData GitHub Issues 联系我们。

English

Privacy Policy

CineCal is a local-first movie calendar and Home Screen widget. We do not require an account, sell personal information, or track users across apps and websites.

Effective: July 14, 2026

Data we do not collect

CineCal does not send names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location, contacts, photos, health data, advertising identifiers, or usage analytics to the developer. The app contains no advertising, third-party analytics SDK, or tracking SDK.

Information stored only on your device

To synchronize the app and widget and improve on-device recommendations, CineCal stores movie identifiers you opened or skipped, the movie selected for the current day, and widget date-display preferences in local storage and the system Keychain. This information is not uploaded to the developer, used for tracking, or shared with third parties.

Network requests and cache

The app downloads its public catalog, recommendation data, vector files, and widget images from the CineCalData GitHub repository and content delivery networks. Images are cached temporarily and automatically pruned to an approximately 20 MB limit. Network providers may process technical information necessary to serve these requests, such as IP address and request time, under their own policies.

Third-party links

When you choose to open a movie card or widget, CineCal opens Douban in its app or website. The third party's privacy policy applies after you leave CineCal.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through CineCalData GitHub Issues.
